FAQ
How long does a typical website project take?
Most websites take 2–4 weeks from kickoff to launch. Landing pages can be done in 3–5 days. Larger projects with custom features typically run 4–8 weeks. I always give you a clear timeline upfront before we start.
What's included in a branding package?
Every branding project includes logo design (multiple concepts), color palette, typography system, brand guidelines document, and all source files (AI, SVG, PNG, PDF). Optional add-ons include business cards, letterheads, and social media kits.
Do you work with clients outside the Philippines?
Absolutely — the majority of my clients are in Canada, the US, and Australia. I'm fully remote and work across time zones. Communication is handled via email, Slack, Zoom, and Loom depending on what works best for you.
How much does a website cost?
It depends on the scope — a landing page starts around $300–$500, while full WordPress websites typically range from $800–$3,000+. I provide a detailed quote after our discovery call so there are no surprises.
Will I be able to update the site myself?
Yes! I build sites so clients can manage them without developer help. I also provide a short walkthrough video after launch so you know exactly how to make updates. And I'm always available if you need help.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
Yes — I offer monthly maintenance packages covering updates, backups, security monitoring, and minor content changes. I don't believe in building something and disappearing. Long-term partnerships are my favourite kind of work.
